FairOSS is a cooperatively and developer-owned movement company that provides an organizational focus for sustaining an Open Source society.

Businesses

A Cooperative Movement

We aim to fill a role similar to the fair trade certification or organic certification for the open source ecosystem.

Equity Badges

We are developing mechanims to ensure that the success of businesses using open source translates into the success of the open source projects they rely on.
